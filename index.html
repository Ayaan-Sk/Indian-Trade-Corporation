Our Legacy

Founded on June 6, 1972 by Khurshid Hussain, Indian Traders Corporation has grown from a specialized trading house into a leading B2B wholesaler for industrial components in central India. For over five decades, we have been the backbone of industries across Vidarbha, MP, CG, and AP.

Now a multi-generational legacy led by Zia Ul Hasan and Mufaddal Hussain, we maintain an annual turnover of ₹10–25 Crore, representing top-tier brands like Yuken, Torque, and Mercury (Aira). Our commitment remains: Precision. Performance. Partnership.
